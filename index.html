How form. Works

Technical Overview

Neural Pipeline
Camera → MediaPipe FaceLandmarker
  ↳ 468 3D facial landmarks
  ↳ Pose detection (yaw/pitch)
  ↳ Blendshape analysis (smile)
PoseLandmarker
  ↳ 33 body keypoints
  ↳ Shoulder & neck alignment
Geometry Engine
  ↳ Gaussian scoring curves
  ↳ DNA baseline calibration
  ↳ Multi-metric weighted sum
All processing: on-device only
Storage: IndexedDB (local)

Gaussian Bell Curve Scoring

Each metric (FWHR, canthal tilt, jaw angle, etc.) is scored using a Gaussian function centered on your DNA baseline — not arbitrary global averages. This means scoring is fair to your unique structure.

DNA Baseline System

On your first scan, form. maps your immutable bone structure. Future scans measure optimization relative to your personal potential — bloat reduction, symmetry, skin clarity — not just bone genetics.

Privacy Architecture

Zero server calls. All vision models are cached locally via a Service Worker. Your face images and scores live only in your device's IndexedDB. No account, no analytics, no uploads.
